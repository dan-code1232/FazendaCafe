Our Story

Family-run. Taste-first.

From Spitalfields to Harrow Place — Fazenda is a family café grounded in Italian coffee rituals and honest, seasonal food. We serve handcrafted espresso, house-baked pastries and deli plates prepared daily.

  • QualitySingle-origin beans & precise extraction
  • FreshnessDaily-baked pastries & seasonal plates
