Our story

A neighbourhood café with Italian heart and local spirit.

Fazenda is a family-run Italian café and coffee shop led by Antonio and Luca, inspired by their earlier Spitalfields location and a love for homemade food, artisan coffee and community hospitality.

This London café honours the warm, informal dining experience of a small Italian deli, bringing authentic coffee, cakes, and fresh deli plates to the heart of East London.

What makes Fazenda different

  • Family-owned café with a relaxed Italian atmosphere.
  • Carefully brewed espresso, flat whites and signature coffee cakes.
  • Classic deli plates, fresh pastries and seasonal light lunches.
  • Art Café creative evenings every Monday with local makers.

Art Café

Open Studio Night at Fazenda

Fazenda becomes a creative space every Monday evening. The Art Café brings people together for art-making, conversation and a relaxed social experience with coffee included.

Tickets are available on Eventbrite or at the café. The session includes all art materials plus a complimentary drink.
