Our story

A family café shaped by flavour and community.

We began in Spitalfields and moved to Harrow Place to bring more neighbours warm espresso, fresh pastries, and simple Italian deli plates. Every item is made with care, hospitality, and the kind of attention that makes guests come back.

Coffee first

Our espresso bar is the foundation of Fazenda. We keep drinks classic, balanced, and approachable so every cup tastes like the real thing.

Food that feels like home

From toasted focaccias to seasonal deli plates and signature cakes, our menu is crafted for guests who want quality flavours without fuss.
