Our story

A cosy Italian corner for coffee, cake and conversation.

Born from the family spirit of Antonio and Luca, Fazenda combines the warmth of a neighbourhood coffee shop with the charm of a classic Italian café. We serve simple, seasonal plates, exceptional coffee and a calm space to meet, work or unwind.

What we love to serve

  • Coffee: Espresso, macchiato, latte, flat white and more.
  • Cakes: Tiramisu cake, coffee cake, fresh pastries and biscotti.
  • Light plates: Deli-style focaccia, soups, salads and lasagna.
  • Events: Monday Art Café creative sessions with materials & drink included.

Why Fazenda feels special

We are small, independent and family-owned. Everything is made with thoughtful ingredients, from our coffee to our cakes. The café is a quiet urban retreat priced for daily visits and friendly enough to feel like a second home.
