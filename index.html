Family Roots

From Spitalfields to Harrow Place.

Brought to London many years ago, Fazenda is an independent, family-run business led by Antonio and Luca. We started our journey in Spitalfields at Leyden Street, pouring our passion for traditional Italian hospitality into every cup.

Today, located just steps from Liverpool Street, we continue to serve rich, aromatic coffees, deli-style lunches, and signature cakes that are nothing short of irresistible.

But we are more than just a café. We believe in community. That’s why our doors stay open on Mondays for artists, thinkers, and friends to gather, create, and connect.

Open Studio Nights

Every Monday evening, Fazenda transforms. We push the tables together, bring out the supplies, and host our open studio sessions. Whether you're into collage, painting, or just want to doodle while sipping a glass of wine with the local community, there is a seat for you.

No prior experience is necessary. We believe everyone has a creative spark that just needs the right environment—and the right espresso—to shine.
